About Me

As a 3D artist and a interaction designer, I have always shared my passion for innovative thinking and creating positive, engaging experiences through my work. With the recent advancements in the digital age, I primarily focused on user experience in immersive 3D experiences with virtual, augmented and mixed realities.

Part of being involved both in the industry and academics, I have had the opportunity to work alongside with other academics and having the opportunity to teach some of the most impressive students out there!

The essential skills that I have developed during my degree at the Design lab at University of Sydney, was being able to think and apply new strategies to further push existing prototypes.
