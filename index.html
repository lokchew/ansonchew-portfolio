About Me As an interaction designer and website developer, I excel in perspective thinking and creating interactive, engaging experiences for users. With a focus on user-interface design and immersive experiences, I leverage emerging technologies to deliver exceptional results.

During my degree at the University of Sydney, I honed essential skills in user research, data analysis, and visualization, as well as user-centred design. I have collaborated effectively with diverse teams and completed various design research projects in the studio units.

With a solid understanding of design theories and a repertoire of diverse research methods, I adapt seamlessly to different project contexts, ensuring optimal and satisfying user experiences. My objective is to leverage my skills and experiences to assist others and expand my horizons.
